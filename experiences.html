GALERI PANTAI TIMUR

After my SPM, i worked as a secretary at Galeri Pantai Timur in Kuantan. It was belong to my mom's friend. I just need
to handle the paintings, events, meetings and keep the place clean. I also learn on how to draw a potrait step by step and
learn more detail about the painting. I got to know a lot of things from my bos also the other artist that came to the
gallery and teach me this and that. I am so grateful because I gain a new knowledge eventhough I am more interesting in
doodles compare to potrait. At least I know the basic so that I can practice more during my leisure time. Sometimes it
quite scary when you were left alone with the paintings because it do feels like the picture was staring at you. Overall, it was
the best working experiences ever.

EVENT MANAGEMENT

During my degree in Semester 1 in Machang, I am the Project Manager for one of the event that have been organized by
Management Subject, under the surveillance of Madam Zaleha Hassan. It was the combination between two classes which
is BA249 and BA242. The program was amazing and everyone do play their role to make the event more enjoyable and
fun. We open the event to other classes as well so they can come and visited our booth. We do have a lot of booth with
different kind of activities and games. This event taught us to managed the booth perfectly and how to make a great team
work with others. It was one of the most memorable moments that we had before we need to flit to Campus Kota Bharu.

HOMSIS 1.0

When I am in Kota Bharu, I have been appointed as Secretary 2 in Islamic Banking Association Club or known as IBAC.
This is one of the programme that I need to handle which is Homsis. During this event, all clubs need to promote their
clubs to the new students to let them know a little bit about your organization. It takes about 3 to 4 days for us to
decorate the booth, to prepare everything that is necessary to make sure the students noticed our booth and also for the
prizes. This event make us know others club members and also get to create new friends. It was one of the enjoyable
moment that we had before Covid came and change everything. But at least, we create the memories with each other.
